# 260 Trust Chain Copy Envelopes

Purpose: define the approximate copy ceilings and verbal posture for `/product`, `/method`, `/proof`, and `/authority`.

## Family Thesis Header
- eyebrow: 1-3 words
- headline: 4-10 words
- thesis support: 10-22 words

Rules:
- thesis first
- no homepage-style sloganization

## Structured Argument Block
- section heading: 2-6 words
- opening paragraph: 20-45 words
- support paragraph: 20-55 words
- optional pull line: 4-12 words

Rules:
- one argumentative move per block
- no wall-of-text
- no expert-primary language in the first paragraph

## Evidence Lens
- lens heading: 2-5 words
- signal line: 6-14 words
- interpretation line: 8-20 words
- support line (optional): 10-22 words

Rules:
- make the evidence legible without dashboard voice
- do not imply product proof that is not currently real

## Architecture Reveal
- concept label: 1-3 words
- explanation stack: 14-36 words
- support line: 8-18 words

Rules:
- hidden complexity should still feel human-readable
- do not turn the page into internal documentation

## Gentle Conversion Block
- heading: 2-6 words
- support line: 8-18 words
- CTA label: 1-3 words

Rules:
- calm next step only
- no sales pressure
