# 261 Homepage Asset Role Envelope

Purpose: define what the homepage assets are allowed to do.

## Hero asset
Primary role:
- category arrival
- biological contact
- unmistakable surface

Must do:
- hold the emotional center of the page
- survive calm copy overlay
- feel alive without becoming spectacle

Must not do:
- explain every family
- carry the whole trust argument
- rely on motion for comprehension

## System Window assets
Primary role:
- support one family truth at a time

Must do:
- reinforce the mechanic or burden of the section
- feel genetically related to the hero world

Must not do:
- become decorative filler
- duplicate the hero’s exact emotional posture in every section

## Lower-spine assets
Primary role:
- support proof and entry calmness

Must do:
- reduce load
- support trust and transition

Must not do:
- escalate visual drama late in the page
- compete with CTA clarity

## Family guidance
- `organism` for category/intelligence
- `state-flow` for continuity, regulation, transition
- `mindform` for density, friction, proof, architecture
- `vessel` for sanctuary, containment, presence
