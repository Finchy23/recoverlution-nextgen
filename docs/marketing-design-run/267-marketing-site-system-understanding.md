# 267 Marketing Site System Understanding

Purpose: summarize what every lane must understand about the marketing site before contributing.

## The whole
The marketing site is not a homepage with extra pages.
It is a public operating surface with a route spine and page families.

## The route spine
### Launch Spine
- `/`
- `/pricing`
- `/enter`

Job:
- create category arrival
- create biological contact
- make the next move calm and obvious

### Trust Chain
- `/product`
- `/method`
- `/proof`
- `/authority`

Job:
- turn intrigue into structured belief

### Support Depth
- `/science`
- `/clinical`
- `/architecture`
- `/research`
- `/claims`

Job:
- support belief without replacing the consumer-facing trust argument

### Transition
- `/auth/start`
- `/auth/callback`
- `/bind`

Job:
- preserve continuity and honesty through commercial/auth steps

## The hierarchy
1. homepage does not explain everything
2. trust pages do not repeat homepage hero burden
3. support-depth pages do not replace trust pages
4. transition pages do not behave like marketing pages

## The philosophy
- the site must feel like one calm intelligence
- complexity belongs under the surface
- the page must carry only its own burden
- proof and authority must feel human-first, not dashboard-first
- design, copy, assets, and frontend all serve the same organism

## The build posture
- clean build
- old homepage is reference only
- unresolved doctrine stays conditional
- captured evidence outranks intention
