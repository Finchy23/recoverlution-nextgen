# 247 Hero / Media Burden Split

Purpose: define what the hero/media system must do and what it must never be forced to do.

## Core rule
Hero media exists to create biological contact, category arrival, and visual inevitability. It does not carry the burden of proving the entire company.

## Hero burden
The hero must deliver:
- category arrival
- emotional recognition
- one unmistakable surface
- one calm intelligence
- first-step desire

The hero must not deliver:
- complete product explanation
- full trust/proof architecture
- all family behaviors at once
- final asset doctrine by itself

## Copy burden
Hero copy must deliver:
- category stamp
- emotional punchline
- restrained proof/support line

Hero copy must not deliver:
- manifesto-length thought
- scientific defense
- investor framing
- multiple CTA arguments at once

## Motion burden
Hero motion must deliver:
- atmosphere
- living intelligence
- continuity
- arrival tension/release

Hero motion must not deliver:
- literal explanation
- heavy narrative sequencing
- dependence on motion for comprehension

## Asset burden
Hero asset must deliver:
- organism-scale identity
- calm power
- recognizability
- crop resilience

Hero asset must not deliver:
- all product families in one image
- all emotional states in one image
- every brand color in one frame

## Supporting system burden
The rest of the homepage must deliver what the hero cannot:
- bridges = pacing and chaptering
- sections 1–3 = operating logic and trust build
- lower spine = proof and entry coherence

## Operational consequence
Do not keep polishing the hero to solve failures that belong to:
- Trust Chain pages
- lower homepage sections
- pricing/enter coherence
- proof/authority pages
