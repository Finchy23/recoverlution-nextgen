# 259 Launch Spine Copy Envelopes

Purpose: define the approximate copy ceilings and verbal posture for Launch Spine modules so the homepage, pricing, and enter surfaces stay calm and non-leaky.

## Category Lockup
- category stamp: 1-2 words
- punchline: 2-5 words
- support line: 6-12 words
- CTA label: 1-3 words

Rules:
- no manifesto paragraphs
- no competitor language
- no scientific defense in the hero zone

## Bridge Pause
- eyebrow: 1-3 words
- punchline: 1-4 words
- accent/support: 4-10 words

Rules:
- it should feel like a breath, not a feature ad
- one idea only

## Family Window
- family label: 1-3 words
- headline: 3-8 words
- support line: 8-18 words
- body (if used): max 28 words
- control/interaction labels: 1-3 words

Rules:
- the mechanic must carry most of the meaning
- copy clarifies burden, it does not explain the whole product

## Trust Proof Band
- statement: 4-10 words
- evidence cue line: 6-14 words
- implication line: 6-16 words

Rules:
- no raw metric dump without interpretation
- no unsupported quantified outcomes

## Commercial Decision Block
- heading: 2-6 words
- support line: 8-18 words
- CTA label: 1-3 words
- optional detail line: 6-14 words

Rules:
- no conversion pressure language
- no fake urgency

## Sanctuary Detail Panel
- heading: 2-5 words
- support line: 8-20 words
- optional stat/label: 1-4 words

Rules:
- support only
- never outshine the dominant module on the page
