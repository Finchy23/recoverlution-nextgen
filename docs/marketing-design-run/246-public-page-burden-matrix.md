# 246 Public Page Burden Matrix

Purpose: define what each public route must carry so we stop overloading single pages and duplicating arguments across the site.

## Primary burden by route

### `/`
Primary burden:
- category arrival
- emotional recognition
- Launch Spine orientation

Must answer:
- what is this
- why does it feel different
- what is the next move

Must not carry:
- full proof argument
- full clinical authority argument
- full architecture explanation

### `/pricing`
Primary burden:
- commercial clarity
- calm choice
- entry expectation

Must answer:
- what am I choosing
- what happens next
- what is conditioned vs available now

Must not carry:
- category explanation from scratch
- deep proof story

### `/enter`
Primary burden:
- continuity posture
- identity/entry reassurance
- honest next step

Must answer:
- where am I in the path
- what do I do next
- what is real vs preview-only

Must not carry:
- pricing argument
- full product education

### `/product`
Primary burden:
- what the system is as an organism
- how the rooms fit together

Must answer:
- what are the major rooms
- how do they reinforce one another
- why this is not a feature bundle

Must not carry:
- full method argument
- full proof argument

### `/method`
Primary burden:
- how change happens in this system

Must answer:
- state before story
- what the loop is
- how the body, proof, and repetition connect

Must not carry:
- full product inventory
- full authority/clinical validation argument

### `/proof`
Primary burden:
- what counts as evidence here

Must answer:
- why proof matters
- what a micro-receipt is
- how one action becomes three meanings

Must not carry:
- broad category education from scratch
- deep clinical positioning as primary burden

### `/authority`
Primary burden:
- why we have the right to speak

Must answer:
- why biology changes the authority model
- why this is not lecture-driven wellness software
- how rigor and restraint coexist

Must not carry:
- full proof system
- full route inventory

### `/science`
Primary burden:
- consumer-safe science explanation

### `/clinical`
Primary burden:
- consumer-safe clinical legitimacy and professional posture

### `/architecture`
Primary burden:
- system structure for depth-seekers

### `/research`
Primary burden:
- evidence lineage and deeper support

### `/claims`
Primary burden:
- explicit statement of what we do and do not claim

## Site-wide rule
If a page is trying to answer more than one primary burden, it will drift toward density, repetition, and loss of conviction.
