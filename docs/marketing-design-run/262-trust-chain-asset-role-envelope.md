# 262 Trust Chain Asset Role Envelope

Purpose: define what assets are allowed to do on `/product`, `/method`, `/proof`, and `/authority`.

## Core rule
Trust Chain assets support belief. They should never replace the argument.

## `/product`
Preferred families:
- `organism`
- `vessel`
- secondary `state-flow`

Role:
- show one ecosystem / one organism
- support room-to-room coherence

## `/method`
Preferred families:
- `state-flow`
- `mindform`
- secondary `vessel`

Role:
- support the loop of change
- support state-before-story

## `/proof`
Preferred families:
- `mindform`
- `state-flow`

Role:
- support proof and transformation without turning into charts

## `/authority`
Preferred families:
- `vessel`
- `mindform`

Role:
- support rigor, restraint, and biological realism

## General prohibitions
- no asset should carry a burden that belongs to copy/structure
- no random family mixing for visual variety alone
- no route should feel like a disconnected aesthetic micro-brand
