RECOVERLUTION: THE UNIVERSAL INTERFACE
(The Architecture of Spatial Presence)
I. THE CORE PHILOSOPHY
When the mind is overwhelmed, options are paralyzing. You do not need a grid of buttons demanding your attention; you need a single point of grounding.

We have completely abandoned the traditional UI matrix. There are no grids, no static tabs, no painted containers, and no notification bells. The entire operating system is reduced to core spatial dimensions on a single, continuous sheet of dark glass.

The Law of the Unbroken Surface (The Death of the Box):
We do not use borders, pills, or painted rectangles to separate information. UI components do not sit on top of the glass; they emerge from within it through localized refraction, blur, and light. Hierarchy is created strictly through typographic proximity, depth of field, and volumetric glow. If a designer can draw a box around an element, it is fundamentally breaking the visual law of the OS.

II. THE SPATIAL BLUEPRINT (The Three-Tier Architecture)
The Universal Player relies on a dynamic, real-time run-string sent directly from the backend. The UI simply morphs to hold the payload perfectly across its spatial dimensions. You do not navigate this system; you shift your state.

1. THE SURFACE (The Default Canvas)
The edge-to-edge glass. The default surface is SYNC—the immediate somatic physics engine, ready to catch you the moment you open the app. The Surface is the stage; the backend is the director. When a session ends, the specific UI melts away, depositing you safely back to this default, grounded canvas.

2. THE ANCHOR (The Somatic Clutch)
The only permanent navigation element on the glass is a centered, beautifully rendered, glowing Orb at the base.

The Radial Invocation: You do not "open a new page." An intentional hold on the Orb causes it to gracefully expand, rippling outward to reveal a borderless half-circle of the core emotional frequencies. You slide your thumb to shift the mode:

TALK: Unspooling the mind.

PLAY: Sonic entrainment.

MOVE: Kinetic release.

REST: Parasympathetic recovery (Forks to READ, SEEK, TUNE).

ECHO: The architecture of proof & infrastructure (Forks to PLOT, ∞MAP, LINK).

3. THE STREAM (The Horizon)
The top slither of the glass. A beautifully rendered, liquid blend of typography.

The Vertical Pull: By rolling the Anchor Orb upward along the Y-axis, the Stream pulls downward, expanding to fill the Z-axis. This is where your FLOW lives. It leads the feed, carrying the next cue of your experiential journey. Beneath the Flow, the Stream seamlessly blends upcoming schedule blocks and quiet affirmations. It acts as a passive compass.

IV. THE SEMANTIC PHYSICS ENGINE
Words are not labels; they are material. Typography in the Universal Player behaves exactly like the WebGL physics—it needs depth, velocity, resistance, and background radiation.

Vocal Threading: A single dumb physics atom can be injected with distinct psychological souls (Companion, Coach, Mirror, Narrator, Activator). The LLM orchestrates the Entrance, the Friction, and the Exit to create a completely bespoke psychological spell.

Multi-Dimensional Material: Text reacts to physical input velocity (Reactive Friction), whispers in the subconscious background (Ambient Subtext), and morphs its meaning the millisecond the physics resolve (Threshold Shift).

The Typographic Bounding Box: To guarantee the design never breaks the glass, the LLM is shackled by strict, mathematically derived character and line-wrap limits injected into the JSON schema. The AI is infinitely creative, but only within the structural dimensions of the UI.

The Delay of Safety: Text must never appear the exact millisecond a screen loads. The user must first absorb the Atmosphere to set their biological safety. Only then does the Signal materialize.

V. THERMODYNAMICS & INDESTRUCTIBILITY
1. Sequence Thermodynamics (The Arc Patterns)
When a user strings multiple interventions together, the OS treats it as a thermodynamic cycle. The Heat Band must mathematically degrade over the sequence (The Law of Cooldown). Sequences are routed through specific emotional shapes to prevent habituation. One cue leaves a particle or trace that seamlessly becomes the seed of the next state (The Carry).

2. Attentional Physics

3. The Fringes (Resilience)

Sensory Override: A simple, grounding gesture instantly strips away all WebGL and haptics, dropping the OS to a pure, flat monochrome for neurodivergent hypersensitivity.

Hardware Symbiosis: If the user's phone hardware is fatigued, the OS algorithms gracefully strip away the heavy volumetric shadows to protect their lifeline to the world.

Acoustic Materiality: The "Foley of the Soul." Digital mass is proven through low-frequency hums and resonant crystal strikes.
